# Capstone Project 2: Customer Segmentation Profiling

## Project Overview
Customer Segmentation Profiling is a Machine Learning and Data Analytics project aimed at grouping customers based on their demographic characteristics, purchasing behavior, and spending patterns. The objective is to create detailed customer profiles that help businesses understand their customers better and develop targeted marketing strategies.

## Problem Statement
Businesses often serve customers with diverse needs and preferences. Treating all customers the same can reduce marketing effectiveness. This project focuses on identifying distinct customer segments and creating profiles that enable personalized engagement and improved customer experiences.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer demographics and behavior
- Identify distinct customer segments
- Develop meaningful customer profiles
- Understand purchasing and spending patterns
- Support targeted marketing and business strategies
